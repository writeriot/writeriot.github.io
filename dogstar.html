Dog Star

by Ello Skelling

Prompt: horror / looking at the stars / “digital” < 100 words

Dr. Walker stood in the teleport, survival suit at full integrity, life support stocked.

He looked at the stars above. Out there, near Sirius, was a habitable planet and he’d just built the first teleporter on Earth. He’d be the first to visit another star system.

He pushed the button.

And opened his eyes to the texture of solid rock. He’d miscalculated the relative positions, arrived underground. Perfectly encased, limbs frozen in place, sharp silicate scratching at his visor, he fought a swelling nausea.

The digital readout in his helmet confirmed that life support was good for another twenty years.
